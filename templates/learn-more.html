                 Scholarships & Discounts
                

















































                 Scholarships and discount offers
                







                  NCI has scholarships for exceptional students both from NCI and other colleges. We also have discount offers for NCI alumni.
                 



































                 International Scholarships:
                
                Please note that many of the following scholarships are not available to students paying international fees.
                
                 National College of Ireland offers
                

                 scholarships for international students
                

                 which aim to promote diversity among our student body.
                





                 Applicants may apply for more than one scholarship (where eligible), however will only be awarded the scholarship of highest value. Scholarships may not be combined or aggregated.
                




                Postgraduate Scholarships
               




































                  Master's Edge Scholarship
                 






                  NCI is offeringa 25%
                  
                   Master's Edge Scholarship
                  
                  for any qualified NCI student or alumni who wishes to progress onto any full-time, day-timeMasters or Postgraduate Diploma programme (not delivered at evenings/weekends) at NCI, commencing in September 2025.
                 




                   Application closing date:
                  
                  August 1st 2025.
                 




                   How to Apply:
                  

                   Master's Edge Scholarship Application Form
                  









                  First-Class Honours Scholarship
                 






                  The First-Class Honours Postgraduate Scholarship is available to all final year undergraduate students who have achieved a first-class honours degree. This scholarship will allow final-year students to avail of a 50% discount on their fees for a part-time or full-time postgraduate programme.
                 



                  This scholarship is available to both NCI and non-NCI graduates.
                 




                   (Please be aware that SUSI would deem this discount an allowance from public funds, and students availing of this discount would not be eligible for a fee grant from SUSI).
                  





                   Application closing date:
                  
                  August 1st 2025.
                 




                   How to Apply:
                  

                   First Class Honours Scholarship Application Form
                  









                  30% Club Scholarship - Full Scholarship
                 






                  Presented in association with
                  
                   The 30% Club
                  
                  , this scholarship addresses the under-representation of women in postgraduate STEM education.
                 



                  Commencing in September,
                  
                   two female candidates
                  
                  will receive fully funded places on any full- or part-time Master's and postgraduate programmes at NCI. There are 2 scholarships available, one for the School of Business and one for the School of Computing.
                 



                  See the
                  
                   30% Club Scholarship guide
                  
                  for more details.
                 




                   Application closing date:
                  
                  May 31st 2025.
                 




                   How to Apply for School of Business Scholarship:
                  

                   30% Club Scholarship for School of Business Application Form
                  





                   How to Apply for School of Computing Scholarship:
                  

                   30% Club Scholarship for School of Computing Application Form
                  









                  1,000 Discount Scheme for NCI Students or Alumni
                 






                  If you are a current undergraduateNCI student, or have graduated in the last number of years from a full level 8 Honours Degree or Higher Diploma, andnow wishto return to complete a part-time Master's or Postgraduate Diploma, we can offer you a possible 1,000 discount off your tuition fees.
                 



                  To avail of this discount, you must have completed your degree and be due to graduate this year or have graduated since September 2011.
                 




                   Application closing date:
                  
                  August 1st 2025.
                 




                   ow to Apply:
                  

                   1,000 Discount Scheme Application Form
                  









                  Refer a Friend Scheme
                 






                  If you are a current part-time postgraduate student, you can gain a 500 discount from your future tuition fees if you successfully refer a friend onto an NCI postgraduate course.
                 




                   More information available here
                  
                  .
                 








                  Irish Refugee Council (IRC) - Postgraduate Full Scholarship (Full-time or Part-time)
                 







                   Irish Refugee Council
                  
                  provides services and support for people seeking protection and people recognised as refugees in Ireland. IRC believes education plays a vital role in peoples development, self-esteem and integration. NCIs mission is to change lives through education. Commencing in September, one candidate, who meets International Protection criteria, is ineligible for States supports, and satisfiesentry requirements for the programme, will receive a full scholarshipto a postgraduate level 9 award, delivered either full-time over 1 year or part-time over 2-years at NCI.
                 




                   Application closing date:
                  
                  May 23rd 2025.
                 




                   How to Apply:
                  

                   IRC Postgraduate Scholarship Application Form
                  









                  Discount for Department of Social Protection Staff
                 






                  National College of Ireland is pleased to offer all Department of Social Protection Staff a 15% discount on any part-time programme for September 2025entry.
                 




                   More information available here
                  
                  .
                 



































                Undergraduate Scholarships
               




































                  Sports Scholarship
                 






                  Leaving Certificate students who have demonstrated excellence in sports are encouragedto apply for
                  
                   NCI's Sports Scholarship
                  
                  .
                 



                  Applications are now open for all prospective students. You can apply through the link above. Should you have any questions please email
                  
                   sport@ncirl.ie
                  
                  .
                 








                  Amundi Scholarship: Supporting Women Into Finance
                 







                   Amundi, the leading European asset manager, and Amundi Ireland Women's Network are offering an academic scholarship to a female student studying the BA (Hons) in Accounting and Finance at NCI. The chosen candidate will receive 5,000 for each year of study (15,000 in total), professional mentoring sessions throughout the duration of their studies and an 8-week paid summer internship at Amundi before their final year.
                  





                   Amundi will fund one undergraduate through to their graduation and then reopen the scholarship for another undergraduate. There will only be one Amundi scholar in any given year.
                  




                  The current Amundi scholar is Jani Doms.
                 








                  Irish Refugee Council (IRC)  Full-time Undergraduate Full Scholarship
                 







                   Irish Refugee Council
                  
                  provides services and support for people seeking protection and people recognised as refugees in Ireland. IRC believes education plays a vital role in peoples development, self-esteem and integration. NCIs mission is to change lives through education. Commencing in September, one candidate, who meets International Protection criteria, has successfully applied for a place via CAO and is ineligible for State supports, will receive full scholarships to an undergraduate level 8 award, delivered full-time over 3 or 4 years at NCI.
                 




                   Application closing date:
                  
                  May 23rd 2025.
                 




                   How to Apply:
                  

                   IRC Undergraduate Scholarship Application Form
                  









                  Uversity - Higher Education Scholarship for Adult Learners
                 






                  NCI is a participating institution in the
                  
                   Uversity Scholarship
                  
                  scheme which isintended to unlock adult learners potential. Uversitys Scholarships support adult learners, who are not otherwise in a financial position to do so, to enrol on and complete a Bachelors degree that will transform their lives and those around them.
                  
                   Uversity Scholarships take account of each individuals circumstances and aim to provide financial support for throughout their undergraduate studies.
                  





                   Payments are made over the course of a degree programme and are subject to continued academic registration and progress. Scholarship amounts vary depending on the successful recipients circumstances and need.
                  





                   Applications for the 2025/26 academic yearare now open. Visit
                   
                    Uversity.org
                   
                   for further information.
