               Master of Business Administration (MBA) at NCI
              



               An MBA will challenge you, inspire you and give you confidence to move forward in your career. Put simply, an MBA is a milestone in both your personal and professional development.
              



               At NCI, our MBA programme will provide you with a strong theoretical foundation in the broader disciplines of management as well as focusing on the practical application of theory in real world settings and cases. You will be exposed to the latest thinking in the core disciplines of enterprise, leadership, organisational management, people management, strategy, decision-making, management of financial resources and economics.
              

















































                   Two Years
                  










                   Part-Time
                  










                   6,700 per annum
                  










                   City Centre
                  
















































                    Apply Online
                   












































































                   Why Choose NCI for your MBA?
                  





                   The MBA at NCI aims to encourage entrepreneurial thinking in our students, a valuable and essential skill in business leadership.
                  






                    Why Choose NCI
                   



















































                   MBA Course Content
                  





                   The course emphasises the development of effective business and management skills to solve management problems through structured analysis, problem solving techniques and effective decision-making.
                  






                    MBA Programme
                   



















































                   MBA Student Profile
                  





                   The MBA students in NCI come from a diverse set of backgrounds, making the course community a rich, varied and interesting one.
                  






                    Learn More

                  Students have enrolled on the MBA Programme since 2011
                 










                  15
                 

                  Nationalities have been represented on the MBA Programme
                 










                  70%
                 

                  MBA holders see their salaries increase by 20% or more in the 2 years following graduation.
                 










                  94%
                 

                  Of MBA students are aged between 30-50+
