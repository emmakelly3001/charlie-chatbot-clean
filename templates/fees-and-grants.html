                 Fees and Grants
                







                  This section contains information about fees for undergraduate and postgraduate courses, along with the various aids, financial supports and payment options available for students.
                 

























































                     Undergraduate Fees
                    

                     Details of the fees for undergraduate courses at NCI for both EU and non-EU students.
                    


















                     Postgraduate Fees
                    

                     Postgraduate course fees for all students on both full-time and part-time courses at National College of Ireland.
                    


















                     Free Fees Initiative
                    

                     Information on the Free Fees Initiative as brought in by the Irish government, including eligibility guidelines.
                    












































































                  Student Laptop Fund
                 





                  Find out if you are eligible for the Government technology grant to assist students with online learning while completing their course at NCI.
                 






                   Find Out More
                  



















































                  Fees Policy
                 





                  Visit the NCI Support Hub for further information on our Fees and Grants Policy and Procedures.
                 






                   Learn More
                  
















































































                     Mandatory Fees
                    



                      Every student must pay the Student Contribution Fee, which goes towards support services, examination fees and registration costs.
                     

















                     Direct Debit Plan
                    



                      A financial plan to pay by direct debit is available for students on eligible courses - click here for details on how to apply and pay via direct debit.
                     

















                     Financial Support
                    



                      If you are experiencing financial difficulties and either thinking of applying to NCI or already studying with us, a number of financial aids may be available to help you.
                     





























































                     Refunds
                    



                      Refunds are only available in certain circumstances, as outlined on this page.
                     

















                     Tax Relief
                    



                      Tax relief on fees up to a designated amount is available at the standard rate of tax.
                     

















                     Scholarships and Discounts
                    



                      A number of scholarships and discounts may be available for students - see this page for details.
